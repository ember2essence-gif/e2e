Our Story

At Ember2Essence, we create handmade candles that are more than light, they are symbols of healing, resilience, and ritual.

Ember2Essence is a purpose-driven candle brand centered on intentional living, nervous system awareness, and meaningful rituals. It creates handmade candles designed to support moments of pause, grounding, and emotional regulation.

Each candle is handmade with care, using premium wax, wooden wick and reusable handmade clay jar.

Beyond the product itself, Ember2Essence seeks to nurture emotional connection with customers through small rituals that encourage wellbeing, self-reflection, and restoration. The brand also partners with social enterprises that provide employment and mentoring opportunities for artists with intellectual and physical disabilities, reinforcing its dedication to inclusivity and community impact.

Founder

Ember2Essence was born from the founder’s personal experiences with sensory overwhelm, emotional fatigue, and the need for grounding rituals in daily life. Lighting a candle began as a small but powerful ritual of pause, a way to slow down, reconnect, and restore balance. This personal practice evolved into a brand built around healing, restoration, and intentional moments of calm.

🕯️ A Better Candle Experience

A few simple steps will help your Ember2Essence candle burn cleaner, last longer, and deliver its full fragrance.

✂️ Before Lighting
  • Trim the wick to 5mm.
  • Place on a heat-resistant surface.
  • Keep away from drafts and flammable objects.
🔥 While Burning
  • Allow the first burn to reach the edges.
  • Burn for up to 2–3 hours.
  • Never leave unattended.
♻️ After Use
  • Stop using when about 10mm of wax remains.
  • Clean and reuse your candle jar.
